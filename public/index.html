Services

When you choose to work with Synappse, expect the best—from the planning stages through to when your application goes live. We take a step-by-step approach towards launch that’s geared toward accounting for every last detail and delivering an exceptional product.

Concept & Plan

During pre-planning, we dig deep to understand your goals and formulate a plan to begin building your system. Direct contact with our developers allows you to clearly communicate what’s working and what isn’t.

Design

We take a great pride in a design process. From day one we work on prototypes and mockups to give you the best experience there is. Based on your feedback, we fine-tune the project and add all those little thing that you will love.

Mobile & Web

Mobile first projects are our favorite. We love building native apps but we are not afraid cross-platform challenges, eg. react-native. For most projects we are developing a website and the backend to feed the app with content.

New Tech

Need to work with iBeacons? Maybe Internet of Things or Virtual and Augmented Reality? We love to play with all the hottest tech out there. Let us know what do you need.

Get in touch

With our passionate, creative team, we can take your project from an idea to a prototype to a full-fledged solution, faster, cheaper, and better than the other guys.
